Resources

Missed the meeting, or just want to brush up on a previous subject? All of our presentations are saved in Google Drive

Netflix

Nova

A PBS TV show that covers a wide range of science topics. The show does a great job of introducing and explaining complex ides such as entanglement and string theory. Their website also has great interactives and can be found here

Cosmos

Neil DeGrasse Tyson revamps the original Cosmos series started by Carl Sagan, aiming to explore the physics and astronomy in daily life.

Youtube

PBS Space Time

Very detailed and in-depth videos about a wide range of topics, such as entanglement and causality. Videos are added regularly.

Veritasium

Thought-provoking videos about physics experiments and philisophy by professor Derek Muller.

Minute Physics

A great starting point for budding astronomers. Short to medium length videos of well explained physics phenomenon and equally helpful animations.

Kurzgesagt

Great animations that explain several fun and interesting topics.

Vsauce

Silly and thought provoking videos that explore in the unique Vsauce style.

Crash Course

Informative 15 minute videos that explore a wide variety of topics.
