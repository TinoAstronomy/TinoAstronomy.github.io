Who are we?

Tino Astronomy is a student based club at Cupertino High School. Tino Astronomy club strives to create a platform where the members can develop a holistic view of the cosmos, and learn about its deeper mechanics. Here, the members will explore reality beginning from the level of the string, traversing all the way up to a grander view of the multiverse system. This open-minded forum not only educates the audience, but also allows them to ponder existing theories, and debate their respective validities. In addition, members can expand their intellectual horizons by participating in prestigious competitions. Meetings are every other Thursday during lunch in room 503.

Email us at cupertinoastronomy@gmail.com or join our Facebook group Tino Astronomy Club
